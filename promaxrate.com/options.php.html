Understanding Options:

Options are financial derivatives that grant the holder the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell an underlying asset at a predetermined price (strike price) before or at the expiration date.

Key Concepts:

Call Options:

Give the holder the right to buy an underlying asset at a specified price before or at expiration.

Put Options:

IProvide the holder the right to sell an underlying asset at a specified price before or at expiration.

Strike Price:

The price at which the option holder can buy or sell the underlying asset.

Expiration Date:

The date when the option contract expires, and the right to exercise the option lapses.
